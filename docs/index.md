noun

ae·​rie | \ ˈer-ē  , ˈir-ē  , ˈā-(ə-)rē  \

variants: or less commonly aery

Definition of aerie

1: the nest of a bird on a cliff or a mountaintop

2: a brood of birds of prey

3: an elevated often secluded dwelling, structure, or position
